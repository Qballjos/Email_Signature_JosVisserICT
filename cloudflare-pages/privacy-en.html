2. What data do we collect?

  • Other data you actively provide

When visiting our website, technical data may be collected, such as:

  • IP address

  • Referring website

3. Why do we use your data?

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Providing ICT services and support
  • Communication: Answering questions and requests
  • Quotations and invoicing: Sending quotes and invoices
  • Customer relationship management: Maintaining business contacts
  • Legal obligations: Complying with fiscal and legal obligations

4. Legal basis

We process personal data based on:

  • Contract: For the execution of our services
  • Consent: When you give explicit consent
  • Legitimate interest: For customer relationship management and business operations
  • Legal obligation: For administrative and fiscal purposes

5. Retention period

We do not retain your personal data longer than necessary for the purposes for which they are collected, or as legally required:

  • Customer data: During the business relationship and up to 7 years after termination (fiscal retention obligation)
  • Correspondence: Up to 2 years after last contact, unless longer needed
  • Quotations: Up to 1 year after sending

6. Sharing data with third parties

Jos Visser ICT never sells your data to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • Suppliers: Such as hosting and email services (only for necessary service delivery)
  • Government agencies: When legally required

All third parties are contractually obligated to treat your data confidentially and only use it for agreed purposes.

7. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure servers and backups
  • Access control and authorization
  • Regular security updates

8. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request which data we process about you
  • Right to rectification: You can have incorrect data corrected
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction: You can have processing restricted
  • Right to data portability: You can receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: You can object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent: When processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, you can contact us at info@josvisserict.nl.
